About Mélanie

Mélanie is from Ottawa, Canada. She studied sociology and criminology at the University of Ottawa and proceeded to work in regulatory affairs as a Safety Management Specialist with Transport Canada. Mélanie's career in regulatory affairs continued to progress over the years and, between 2011 and February 2019, she was Vice-President of Regulatory Affairs and Safety with the Canadian Propane Association.

Passionate about the Mayan Riviera, her move to Playa del Carmen was a natural fit! Mélanie first visited the region in 2002 and immediately fell in love. After yearly visits, in 2016, Mélanie and her spouse decided to make their dream a reality. They purchased a lot in Playa del Carmen and hired a team of experts to help them build their custom home.

In early 2019, Mélanie decided to take the plunge and embarked on a new career path. Her hard work, charisma and true passion to help others achieve their realty dreams has helped Mélanie's reputation as a real estate expert quickly rise. Whether you are looking for an investment opportunity or a place to call home, Mélanie will guide you to find the perfect property!

Mélanie is fluent in French and English. She also has a good command of Spanish.
